Information and Roadmap

jko was born out of the need to organize and share scripts and dependencies between projects‚Äîa gap that, after some research, I found was left for external tools by the npm team about 10 years ago, rather than being integrated directly into package.json (I believe a solution like allowing a package.js, as eslint does, would have been preferable).
